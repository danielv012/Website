Research

Autonomous Driving

I'm the project leader of Nova, which is UT Dallas's premier autonomous driving research group. Nova consists of undergraduate students from a variety of academic backgrounds, working in tandem under the Applied Systems lab of Assoc. Prof. Justin Ruths. Our goal is to develop and deploy the first end-to-end, modular, open-source autonomous driving stack for use by researchers, hobbyists, and/or educational institutions. We also aim to provide autonomous shuttle services to the UT Dallas community.
